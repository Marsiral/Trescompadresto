About Us

We are not here to make a statement nor to be part of conversations. We are here to set standards and start conversations. Tres Compadres was started in 2020 with the idea of making our Mexican dishes known and this starts with the taco. Compadre is a word used to describe the relationship between parents and their children’s godparents, for us, our child is our restaurant and we are here with the same love for it, to care for it and to help it grow.

Hospitality has always been more than a job to us, it’s a lifestyle. We do it because we care for people’s happiness and we at Tres Compadres want to be able to bring more smiles on faces. Simplicity, authenticity and consistency is what we thrive on, and our food speaks for itself.
